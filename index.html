Jalfonze

Singer/Songwriter in Berlin

"But we both know, we're Driving Down a Dead End"

A Philippine-born musician that was raised in England, Jalfonze is currently residing in Berlin to focus on his craft as a songwriter. Inspired by the sounds of soul and indie pop, his music can often be described as a combination of the two, with a hint of bluesy vocal lines and a splash of RnB-esque guitar harmony. With a flirtatious stage presence as well as being an avid performer within the Berlin music scene, he was normally found in late night live music bars to showcase his original songs, and interesting stage approaches to make sure that each performance was one to remember. Jalfonze has currently released 3 singles which can be found on all music streaming sites.
